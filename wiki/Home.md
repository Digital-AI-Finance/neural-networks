# Neural Networks Wiki

Welcome to the Neural Networks course wiki. This wiki provides condensed concept explanations organized by course part.

## Course Overview

**Neural Networks: From Brain to Business - How Machines Learn to Predict**

This course takes you from the biological inspiration of neurons through to practical business applications in financial market prediction.

## Learning Objectives

Upon completion, you will be able to:

1. Explain biological neuron to artificial neuron mapping
2. Calculate neuron outputs using weighted sums and activations
3. Design appropriate network architectures
4. Trace forward propagation through a network
5. Understand gradient descent learning
6. Diagnose overfitting and underfitting
7. Evaluate models using confusion matrices
8. Assess trading strategy performance via backtesting

## Prerequisites

- Basic algebra (equations, functions)
- Basic statistics (mean, probability)
- No programming required
